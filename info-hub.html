About FreshKart

FreshKart is India's fastest-growing online grocery delivery platform, committed to delivering fresh, high-quality products directly to your doorstep. Founded in 2020, we have served over 500,000+ happy customers across Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Chennai, and Kolkata.

Our Mission

To make fresh, healthy, and affordable groceries accessible to every Indian household.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted grocery delivery platform by 2028.

Our Impact

  • 500,000+ Happy Customers
  • 50,000+ Daily Deliveries
  • 100% Farmer Direct Sourcing
  • 98% On-Time Delivery Rate

Our Values

  • Quality First: Every product goes through rigorous quality checks.
  • Customer Obsession: Your satisfaction is our top priority.
  • Transparency: No hidden charges, no misleading information.
  • Sustainability: Eco-friendly packaging and reduced carbon footprint.
